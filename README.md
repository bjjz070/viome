## What Is Viome, Actually?

Viome is a personalized health company founded in 2016 by Naveen Jain out of Bellevue, Washington. The company raised over $175 million in its first two years, receiving support from investors like Khosla Ventures and Bold Capital. The core idea is straightforward: send in a sample (or a few), let their lab crunch the data, and get back a personalized roadmap for your health.

What separates Viome from the pack is *how* they test. Viome offers an at-home RNA gut and health test that measures users' microbial, human, and mitochondrial gene expressions to offer personalized food and supplement recommendations. That's RNA sequencing, not DNA. Unlike many other services that rely on DNA â€” which is unchangeable â€” Viome looks at mRNA, which is constantly changing, and provides a snapshot of your current health status. That's actually a meaningful difference â€” it catches what's happening *right now* in your gut, not just what you were born with.

The company has tested over one million microbiomes at this point, and the company has partnered with esteemed institutions like the Mayo Clinic. Their lab is CLIA-certified, which is the same standard clinical labs in hospitals have to meet.
